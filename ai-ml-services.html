Overview

As a principal investigator, you want to get faster approvals and start your research quickly. For this it is important to ensure when creating protocols that all information is captured correctly and in sync.

As a principal investigator, you want to be compliant with IACUC regulations and flag any discrepancies or potential violations in animal research protocols.

As a manager tasked with managing animal facilities, you want to optimize resource allocation, track inventory levels, and predict maintenance needs, ensuring efficient operations and optimal animal care.

AI can make this happen.

AI algorithms enhance the process of protocol creation by extracting and analyzing information, ensuring that all relevant data, such as history, objectives, risks, and other critical information, is captured accurately and in sync. This not only streamlines the protocol creation process but also minimizes errors and inconsistencies, expediting the approval process and enhancing research integrity.

AI-powered analytics can monitor and ensure compliance with IACUC regulations, flagging any discrepancies or potential violations in animal research protocols.

Implementing AI solutions for facility management can optimize resource allocation, track inventory levels, and predict maintenance needs, ensuring efficient operations and optimal animal care.

AI algorithms can analyze real-time animal health data, identifying potential health issues early and enabling proactive interventions to maintain animal welfare and research integrity.
